Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my thumbnails?

We offer 24-hour delivery for all standard thumbnail packages. Rush delivery options are also available for an additional fee if you need your thumbnails urgently.

What information do you need to create my thumbnails?

We'll need your video title, any specific elements you want included, your preferred style, brand colors if you have them, and any reference images for the overall look you're aiming for.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we offer up to 2 rounds of revisions for each thumbnail to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design. Our goal is your complete satisfaction.

Can you match my existing channel branding?

Absolutely! We'll study your channel's existing style and create thumbnails that perfectly match your branding while still optimizing for click-through rate.

What formats do you deliver?

We deliver all thumbnails in high-resolution PNG format (1280x720 pixels), optimized for YouTube's specifications. Other formats available upon request.
